タクティク リファレンス

  • 証明の補助: idtac, fail, move, clear, set, remember, pose, rename, intro, assert, cut, lapply, specialize, generalize
  • 項による証明: exact, refine, apply
  • コンストラクタの適用: constructor, split, exists, left, right
  • 否定と矛盾: absurd, contradict, contradiction, exfalso
  • 帰納法と場合分け: fix, cofix, elim, induction, case (case_eq), destruct, intros, decompose, inversion
  • 等式: reflexivity, symmetry, transitivity, decide equality, compare, discriminate, injection, congruence, f_equal, simplify_eq
  • 存在変数の扱い: evar, instantiate, …
  • 式の変形・書き換え: cbv, lazy, compute, red, hnf, simpl, pattern, unfold, fold, rewrite, replace, subst, change
  • 証明の自動化: assumption, auto, trivial, tauto, intuition, rtauto, firstorder, omega, ring, ring_simplify, field, field_simplify, fourier, autorewrite
